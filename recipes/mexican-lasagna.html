Mexican Lasagna - Without Noodles!

Description

This is a traditional dish in my family. Anyone can make it really. It is composed of traditional Mexican ingredients layered over the top of one another in a lasagna-like fashion (hence the name) but has little similarity to lasagna, so lasagna haters have no fear!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Water
  • Taco Seasoning 1 packet will do
  • Tortillas Use as many as desired
  • Refried Beans This lasagna recipe is flavored with classic Mexican refried beans
  • Shredded Colby-Jack Cheese 4 cups will do
  • Taco Sauce Make this lasagna extra decadent.

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch casserole dish.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and stir in ground beef. Cook and stir until beef is crumbly, evenly browned, and no longer pink, about 10 minutes.
  3. Drain and discard any excess grease. Add water and taco seasoning; cook and stir until mixture has thickened, 5 to 10 minutes.
  4. Arrange 3 tortillas in the bottom of the casserole dish; spread about half the can of refried beans and half the ground beef atop the tortilla layer.
  5. Sprinkle 1 cup Colby-Jack cheese over ground beef layer. Arrange 3 more tortillas over the cheese.
  6. Spread in the remaining 1/2 can refried beans and half the jar of taco sauce; sprinkle with 1 cup Colby-Jack cheese.
  7. Arrange 3 more tortillas and top with remaining ground beef and 1 cup Colby-Jack cheese. Top with last 3 tortillas and remaining 1/2 jar taco sauce.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until top tortilla layer begins to brown, 20 to 25 minutes. Sprinkle 1 cup Colby-Jack cheese over lasagna.
  9. Turn on oven's broiler
  10. Place lasagna under the broiler until Colby-Jack cheese is melted and bubbling, 3 to 5 minutes.
